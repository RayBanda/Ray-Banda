HELLO MY NAME IS RAY
AND I CAN HELP WITH ALL YOUR MOTION NEEDS

I am a freelance motion graphics designer, food lover and humorous person living far away in Perú.

After graduating design school I've worked in local design agencies, found great joy in creating motion graphics and chose to specialize in it. I've collaborated with awesome agencies, involving projects for brands such as: Omo, FBS,  KAIA, Wordpress and many others smaller brands.

 

I also create weekly tutorials and tips for Instagram and Youtube, these focus on the process and most importantly how every tool we use works, so we understand what's happening step by step.

So, if there is any opportunity or project in your mind, don't hesitate and contact me in any of my social media accounts, I am more than happy to hear from you, and of course, collaborate and grow together.
